Our Strategic Workstreams

Five interconnected pillars driving UM6P's impact across Africa through strategic coordination and collaboration.

WS 1: Envisioning African futures in science, technology & education

Developing forward-thinking strategies and frameworks to guide Africa's advancement in science, technology, and educational excellence for future generations.

WS 2: Aligning R4D with development priorities

Ensuring research for development (R4D) initiatives are strategically aligned with Africa's most pressing development needs and priorities.

WS 3: Fostering academic excellence through partnerships and Networks

Building strategic academic partnerships and networks to enhance research capabilities and foster collaborative excellence across institutions.

WS 4: Connecting the dots on innovation & entrepreneurship

Creating synergies between innovation initiatives and entrepreneurial ventures to build a comprehensive ecosystem for sustainable development.

WS 5: Communication and outreach

Developing comprehensive communication strategies and outreach programs to amplify UM6P's impact and engage diverse stakeholders across Africa.

Partnership Philosophy

We believe that Africa's greatest strength lies in its diversity of perspectives, experiences, and innovations. Our approach to partnerships is built on mutual respect, shared learning, and collaborative problem-solving.

By connecting institutions, researchers, entrepreneurs, and policymakers across the continent and beyond, we create an ecosystem where knowledge flows freely, innovations emerge organically, and solutions are designed by and for African communities.
