Our Collaborators

ARUA

Collaborating with ARUA to promote research excellence and foster collaboration among African universities in addressing shared challenges.

AAU

Engaging with the Association of African Universities (AAU) to support academic partnerships and contribute to the development of higher education across Africa.

RUFORUM

Working alongside RUFORUM to strengthen research and education capacity in agriculture and sustainable development across African universities.

ACE IMPACT

Partnering with ACE IMPACT to help advance postgraduate education and research in STEM, supporting innovation and development across the continent.

OCP Africa

Collaborating with OCP Africa to contribute to empowering smallholder farmers through agricultural solutions and tailored training programs.

FAO

Working with FAO to support efforts addressing food security and sustainable agriculture, fostering resilience in African communities.

Agripreneur Alliance

Collaborating with Agripreneur Alliance to encourage and support young agricultural entrepreneurs, promoting innovation in agribusiness.

RESCIF

Partnering with RESCIF on addressing future challenges in key areas such as water, energy, and nutrition.
