Launch of Africa Futures Forum

February 25, 2025 UM6P Team

UM6P hosts its first forum to envision Africa’s tech future, bringing together 100+ researchers and leaders to discuss innovation scenarios for 2050. The Africa Futures Forum marks a significant milestone in our mission to foster collaboration and innovation across the continent.

A Vision for 2050

The forum featured keynote speeches from renowned experts in technology, education, and sustainable development. Topics ranged from artificial intelligence in agriculture to renewable energy solutions tailored for African contexts. Over three days, participants engaged in workshops, panel discussions, and networking sessions to shape a roadmap for Africa’s future.

One of the key outcomes was the establishment of the Africa Futures Network, a collaborative platform that will connect researchers, policymakers, and innovators to drive impactful projects. This network will be supported by UM6P’s research for development (R4D) initiatives and OCP’s on-the-ground expertise.

Highlights from the Event

Here are some highlights from the forum:

  • Day 1: Opening ceremony with a keynote on "Technology as a Catalyst for African Development."
  • Day 2: Workshops on AI, blockchain, and sustainable agriculture, with a focus on scalability.
  • Day 3: Closing session announcing the Africa Futures Network and future collaboration opportunities.

We’re excited to see how these initiatives will shape the continent’s trajectory over the coming decades. Stay tuned for updates on the Africa Futures Network and upcoming events!
