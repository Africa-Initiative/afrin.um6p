For Africa: UM6P’s Commitment to Industry, Research, and Business Innovation

January 10, 2025 UM6P Team

At Mohammed VI Polytechnic University (UM6P), excellence is not just a goal—it’s a mission. Based in Benguerir, Morocco, UM6P is redefining what it means to be a leader in education, research, and innovation across Africa. Through its specialized institutes like the African Academy of Industrial Training (AAIT), the Africa Institute for Research in Economics and Social Sciences (AIRESS), the Africa Business School (ABS), and the Center for African Studies, UM6P is equipping the continent with the skills, knowledge, and vision needed to address global challenges and drive sustainable development. These initiatives underscore UM6P’s commitment to fostering excellence in industry, research, and business innovation, positioning Africa as a powerhouse of progress on the world stage.

African Academy of Industrial Training (AAIT): Empowering Industry with World-Class Skills

UM6P’s strategy for advancing professional training in the industrial sector takes shape through the African Academy of Industrial Training (AAIT), an initiative designed to become a world-class leader in industrial education. AAIT is dedicated to equipping both national and continental industries with cutting-edge skills and competencies to meet the demands of global competitiveness. The academy achieves this through a multifaceted approach:

  • Program Development: Offering continuous and certifying training programs tailored to the industrial sector’s needs.
  • Recruitment Support: Assisting industrial partners with recruitment strategies by providing targeted training that aligns with workforce requirements.
  • Customized Diplomas: Designing diploma programs that meet the specific demands of the industrial sector.
  • Advisory Services: Providing industrial partners with advisory services, customized training, and skill development curriculums.
  • Economic Ecosystem Development: Fostering innovation in the industrial field to contribute to the growth of economic ecosystems across Africa.

To ensure proximity to its clients, AAIT operates in four strategic locations across Morocco: Benguerir, Khouribga, Jorf Lasfar, and Safi. This geographical presence allows the academy to deliver hands-on, industry-relevant training that empowers professionals to drive innovation and competitiveness in their fields.

Africa Institute for Research in Economics and Social Sciences (AIRESS): Shaping Public Policy Through Rigorous Research

At the forefront of UM6P’s research endeavors in the social sciences is the Africa Institute for Research in Economics and Social Sciences (AIRESS), housed within the Social Sciences, Economics, and Humanities Cluster. AIRESS is a hub for groundbreaking research, focusing on critical public policy issues in Africa, including ecology, sustainable development, education, decision analysis, economic policy, social policy, security, diplomacy, and international relations.

AIRESS serves as a collaborative space where professors from the Faculty of Governance, Economics, and Social Sciences (FGSES), along with researchers and doctoral students, work together on programs that address the disciplinary areas taught at UM6P. These programs include targeted research projects led by dedicated teams of scholars who employ the most rigorous scientific methods. The research has two primary objectives:

  1. Identifying and generating actionable data to inform public policy issues in the Global South.
  2. Publishing findings from both fundamental and applied research in high-ranking academic journals, contributing to the global body of knowledge.

Through its focus on evidence-based research, AIRESS is shaping the future of public policy in Africa, providing policymakers with the tools and insights needed to address complex societal challenges.

Africa Business School (ABS): Transforming Business and Society Through Lifelong Learning

The Africa Business School (ABS), the business school of UM6P, is dedicated to creating sustainable impact and enabling organizational transformation across Africa and beyond. ABS positions itself as an inclusive hub for lifelong learners and mindful doers, seamlessly integrating research, advisory services, and learning to drive change in the business and societal landscape.

ABS promotes advanced decision-making and enriched learning capabilities by combining scientific disciplines with management principles. This interdisciplinary approach equips leaders to confidently address global challenges in an increasingly complex world. Looking toward the future, ABS leverages cutting-edge innovations and technologies to prepare its students and partners for the opportunities of tomorrow. Whether through executive education, research initiatives, or advisory services, ABS is committed to transforming the way businesses operate in Africa, fostering a culture of sustainability and innovation.

Center for African Studies: Understanding Africa from Its Own Perspective

The Center for African Studies, a teaching and research center of Mohammed VI Polytechnic University in Benguerir, Morocco, is devoted to studies relating to the African continent. It focuses on fostering a deeper understanding of Africa through three key objectives:

  • Promoting Studies on the Continent: Encouraging and supporting research initiatives conducted within Africa to highlight local perspectives and solutions.
  • Documenting External Studies: Compiling and analyzing studies about Africa produced globally to create a comprehensive knowledge base.
  • Global Perspective from Africa: Teaching studies that enable a view of the world through Africa’s unique lens, fostering a deeper global understanding.

The Center emphasizes five key areas of research: Anthropology, Environment, Health, History, and Philosophy. Through these focus areas, the Center for African Studies contributes to UM6P’s mission of advancing knowledge and innovation that is deeply rooted in African contexts.

A Unified Vision for Excellence

Through AAIT, AIRESS, ABS, and the Center for African Studies, UM6P is building a comprehensive ecosystem that bridges industry, research, business innovation, and cultural understanding. These initiatives reflect UM6P’s broader mission to empower Africa’s youth, professionals, and leaders with the skills, knowledge, and opportunities needed to drive progress. By fostering excellence in these critical areas, UM6P is not only addressing Africa’s immediate challenges but also positioning the continent as a global leader in innovation, sustainability, and economic development.

UM6P’s commitment to excellence is a call to action for all who seek to make a difference. Whether you’re an aspiring industrial professional, a researcher passionate about public policy, a business leader aiming to transform your organization, or a scholar interested in African studies, UM6P offers the resources and support to help you succeed.
